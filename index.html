Community-Centered Hubs

Jodidaar is more than an app—it's a movement. We're setting up sector-wise hubs with local resources, creating places where subscribers find connection, safety, and care.

Trusted Medical Partnerships

We maintain a panel of certified ambulances and hospitals, ready to respond. These trusted partners form the emergency backbone of every hub.

Personalized Safety Profiles

Subscribers share medical history, allergies, and insurance info (self-bought or state-provided). We keep it current and link it to help when it’s needed most.

Care Coordination, End-to-End

When a distress alert is received, we link relevant services, update family, and stay involved until the subscriber is safe again. No hand-offs, just oversight and care.

The Jodidaar Solution

Total Companion Model:

  • Companionship: AI-powered presence
  • Monitoring: Continuous location & behavior check-ins
  • Rescue: Smart alerts + real SAR coordination

How It Works

  1. Onboarding: Set your preferences and contacts
  2. Monitoring: Device + app monitor you passively
  3. Trigger: Fall, silence, or panic event detected
  4. Rescue: Escalation and oversight start instantly

Technology Stack

  • LoRa, BLE, GPS, motion sensors
  • AI/ML for behavioral anomaly detection
  • Zephyr RTOS + embedded resilience
  • Cloud SAR backend + smartphone sync

Use Cases

  • 🏔️ Solo hikers & trekkers
  • 👵 Elderly at home or alone
  • 🌪️ Disaster zones & field workers
  • 🚶‍♀️ At-risk individuals (LGBTQ+, migrants, women)
  • 🚗 Vehicle-based travel (buses, cars, aircraft)

Business Model

  • Free app for everyone
  • Hardware device (optional, low-cost)
  • Premium subscription for rescue plan
  • Partnerships with NGOs & governments

Why Now?

  • More people living, working, and traveling alone
  • Mental health and social isolation are worsening
  • Tech is finally smart, cheap, and scalable enough
